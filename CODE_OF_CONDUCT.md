# Code of Conduct

Project spaces should be welcoming, constructive, and safe for everyone.

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully, critique ideas rather than people, accept feedback in good faith, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, or publication of another person’s private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially and promptly as practical.

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and public interactions representing the project.
